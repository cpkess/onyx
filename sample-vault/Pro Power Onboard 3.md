Pro Power Onboard (PPO) is a system designed to convert a Ford vehicle into a mobile power source, allowing it to serve as a backup for essential home needs during power outages [[Pro Power Onboard Ford.com]].

## Home Backup Capabilities
When paired with a GenerLink transfer switch (sold separately), the Pro Power Onboard™ system can deliver essential home backup during power outages. This capability is utilized when the system is used with an F-150® Hybrid PowerBoost™ or F-150® Lightning® truck equipped with the available Pro Power Onboard™ system.
